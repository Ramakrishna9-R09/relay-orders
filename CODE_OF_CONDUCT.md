# Code of Conduct

Relay Orders is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free
community.

Contributors are expected to communicate constructively, assume good intent,
focus criticism on ideas and code, and respect different backgrounds and
experience levels. Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and
publication of private information are not acceptable.

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior harms the
community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through their GitHub
profile. Reports will be reviewed fairly and kept confidential where possible.
